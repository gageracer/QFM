About

Mentorship Project?

Mentorship system, being used by leading universities worldwide, is implemented in various ways by educational institutions.

University Mentorship Program?

Students will be able to plan for the path they need to follow to accomplish their goals by receiving guidance on many subjects such as information about departments/programs; receiving support for their skills and talent; getting help with identifying their careers, studying abroad; gaining experience in social, cultural, academic and professional areas.

Career Mentorship Program?

The purpose of the Project co-organized by IUE Alumni Relations Office and IUE Career Guidance Center is to bring IUE students and alumni together, ensure graduates share their career and work experiences with students, and to prepare the students to work life best way possible in addition to help increase their cultural and academic developments.

Services

Career Mentorship

Mentor Profile

Mentor needs to have leadership skills, have good command of Izmir University of Economics values and eager to relay them, have positive attitude, be good at crisis management, and be creative and energetic.

Mentor needs to provide support on different subjects. First of all, a mentor, in the eye of the student, will be a teacher; will inform the students about new approaches and share own experiences. Mentor will also build a friendship with the student.

Who Can Become a Mentor

Mentors need to have at least three years of work experience due to the fact that personal experience is fundamental in this program.

All mentors need to have completed undergraduate and/or graduate studies at Izmir University of Economics.

They need to have good problem solving, leadership, and communication skills.

What is Expected of Mentors

Listen to the wants and expectations of students

Provide insights into best business practices

Provide support in researches, project implementations, and search for resources

Provide insights into interviews

Inform students about sectors

Participate in activities to get to know mentees

Dedicate at least 4 hours a month to this project

Who Can Become a Mentee

Students of Izmir University of Economics, who are at least in 3rd grade, are all prospective Career Mentees.

What is Expected of Mentees

Present their wants and expectations clearly

Be open to criticisms, be able to revise goals

Comprehend the significance of the program

University Mentorship

Mentor Profile

Mentor needs to have leadership skills, have good command of Izmir University of Economics values and eager to relay them, have positive attitude, be good at crisis management, and be creative and energetic.

Mentor needs to provide support on different subjects. First of all, a mentor, will inform the students about new approaches and share own experiences. Mentor will also build a friendship with the student.

Who Can Become a Mentor

Mentors need to be at least in 2nd grade or above

They need to be a member of at least 2 student clubs

They need to be actively involved in University’s social activities

They need to have a minimum GPA of 2.5. They need to have good command of university life, life in Izmir, communication, and socialization.

What is Expected of Mentors

Listen to the wants and expectations of students

Help them adapt to life on campus

Help them adapt to city life

Provide support in researches, course implementations, and search for resources

Participate in activities to get to know mentees

Encourage them to get socialized

Who Can Become a Mentee

Students, who enrolled to Izmir University of Economics recently, are all prospective University Mentees.r

What is Expected of Mentees

Present their wants and expectations clearly

Be open to criticisms, be able to revise goals

Comprehend the significance of the program

Benefits

Benefits of Becoming a Mentor

Mentorship is a powerful personal development and empowerment tool.

It will improve responsibility and leadership traits. Today, in many workplaces, the most concrete example to leadership and responsibility is being a successful Mentor.

Participate in “Mentorship Trainings”.

It allows mentors and mentees to make useful networking connections.

Successful Mentors will be issued certificates at the end of academic year based on evaluations.

Benefits of Becoming a Mentee

Career Mentees (3rd and 4th grade IUE students):

This program supports personal development of Mentee and Mentor mutually.

Mentee will have a better grasp of academic life; find a professional support for projects and research quests.

Mentee will get a chance to review career strategies and goals, analyze them with a different perspective and detect any short comes.

Experience is the knowledge or mastery of an event or subject gained through involvement in or exposure to it; however, some experiences have significant outcomes for individuals. This program will allow Mentee to learn and assimilate Mentor’s Professional experiences.

This will all result in enrichment of individual’s potential, and increase in self-confidence and qualities.

University Mentees (new IUE students):

This program supports personal development of Mentee and Mentor mutually.

Mentee will have a better grasp of university life; find a professional support for projects and research quests, and accelerate adapting to university.

Experience is the knowledge or mastery of an event or subject gained through involvement in or exposure to it; however, some experiences have significant outcomes for individuals. This program will allow Mentee to learn and assimilate Mentor’s Professional experiences.
